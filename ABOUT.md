# About Inkrypt 🖋️

Inkrypt is a privacy-first, zero-knowledge personal diary application designed for those who value absolute digital sovereignty. Originally born as "Secret Capsule", it has evolved into a robust, cross-platform tool focused on high-grade security and a premium writing experience.

## The Mission
Our goal is to provide a "digital bunker" for your thoughts. In an era of pervasive data harvesting, Inkrypt ensures that your most personal reflections remain yours and yours alone.

## Core Philosophy
1. **Zero-Knowledge**: We never see your password. We never see your notes. We cannot decrypt your data even if compelled by law.
2. **Local-First, Cloud-Optional**: Your data is always stored and encrypted on your device first. Synchronization is done via encrypted blobs that look like noise to the server.
3. **Open Source**: Trust must be verifiable. Our code is licensed under the AGPLv3, ensuring its freedom and transparency forever.
4. **Premium UX**: Privacy software doesn't have to be ugly. We combine high-security primitives with a modern, fluid interface.

## The Team
Inkrypt is maintained by a team of security-obsessed developers dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of code quality and cryptographic integrity.
