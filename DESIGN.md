# Inkrypt — Design System & Visual Philosophy 🎨

Inkrypt is designed with a "Digital Bunker" aesthetic — a clean, modern, and dark-mode-first interface that prioritizes focus and security.

## 🎨 Color Palette (Futuristic Dark)
- **Primary**: Indigo (#6366f1) — Used for primary CTAs and brand highlights.
- **Secondary**: Slate (#1e293b) — Deep, dark backgrounds that minimize eye strain.
- **Accent**: Emerald (#10b981) — Success states and trust indicators.
- **Surface**: White (#ffffff) on Landing; Glassmorphism on the Dashboard.

## 📐 Layout Principles
1. **Focus Mode**: When writing, we hide navigation sidebars to maximize vertical space.
2. **Micro-interactivity**: Smooth hover states and transitions for tags, buttons, and form labels.
3. **Accessibility (a11y)**: Every interactive element has high-contrast focus rings and aria-labels for screen readers.

## 🖋 Typography
- **Core Font**: Inter (via Google Fonts) — highly legible and modern.
- **Writing Area**: Lora (Serif) or Inter (Sans-Serif) — user-toggleable for a personalized writing experience.
- **Code**: JetBrains Mono — used for the rich text editor's inline code blocks.
